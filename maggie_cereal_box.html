Cereal Box

It is an interesting project that I designed for a particular “client” —John Snow in Game of Throne. Consider John Snow’s personality, I pick three main color palettes for this design: black, white, and gold. In the back side of the cereal box is The Night’s Watch‘s declaration. In general, it would be a cool peripheral product under Game of Throne marketing.
