Album design

This album design was made for one of my favorite bands Real Estate’s new album In Mind. Their music style gives me the feeling like breeze blow to me and makes me enjoy it. The lazy tone sounds like a cat slowly and lightly walking toward me and looking at me with it’s bright eyes. That’s how I got inspiration from their album and designed it by Adobe Illustrator. I created many palettes by my own and applied it on the light to create more textures and sense of depth. In addition, it is more interesting to look at.
